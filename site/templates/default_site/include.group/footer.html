About me

I’m Steve McKinney, a Web Designer living in Manchester, England. I've been designing from fairly young age and doing it ‘in industry’ for the past 3 years. I couldn’t see myself doing anything else. I can start a website from paper, or just jump straight into Photoshop, it all leads to what I feel is the best result. I try to justify everything on a website, if it offers no value, I get rid of it. I’m good with the usual stuff HTML, CSS, & Photoshop, with some JavaScript. I’m familiar with Wordpress and ExpressionEngine. The aim with this website is to be more of a place to share what I learn, and to use it to spur me on to get better at what I do through blogging.
