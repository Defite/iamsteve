About me

I’m Steve McKinney, a web designer living in Manchester, England. I've been designing from young age and doing it ‘in industry’ for the past 4 years. My passion is with design and I learnt to code out of necessity. However, I believe code can give you an advantage, to control your ideas, and communicate them in a whole new way.

In all tutorials that I write I aim to provide understanding and rationale, as to why something is done a particular way. When designing and writing code, I believe this is crucial to long term understanding. If you have any feedback or need clarification, feel free to contact me.
