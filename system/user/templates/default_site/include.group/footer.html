About me

I’m Steve McKinney, a Web Designer living in Manchester, England. I've been designing from fairly young age and doing it ‘in industry’ for the past 4 years. My passion is with design, though I learnt to code out of necessity. Although at the time I thought it was just part of making a website.

My aim for this website, is, for it to be a place to share my knowledge, and for people who cover a similar role to myself. So if you design and code, I’m sure you will find some value in the content. I cover topics around design, website performance and Wordpress. I cover these topics mostly at an intermediate level, but there will be beginner and advanced topics occasionally. If you ever have any feedback or things you struggle with tweet or contact me.
